Frequently asked questions

What if the agent doesn't save me anything?

You pay nothing extra. For pay-per-call users, we refund the $5 if the agent hits your walk-away without a deal. For subscribers, that call counts against your limit but you're still covered by the flat subscription.

Is this legal? Will the company know it's AI?

Yes, fully legal. The agent discloses it's an AI voice assistant at the start of every call and gets rep consent before proceeding. CFPB guidelines permit AI-assisted calls with proper disclosure. We've tested this across 23 providers with zero complaints.

Will this hurt my relationship with the provider?

No. The agent is trained to be polite, professional, and to end calls gracefully when targets aren't met. Comcast reps log a "positive interaction" flag after successful calls — this actually makes future negotiations easier.

How do I know the agent won't accept bad terms?

It can't. Your walk-away price is a hard floor — the agent is programmed to end the call before accepting any offer below it. You review the strategy before every call and can whisper instructions or take over at any time.
